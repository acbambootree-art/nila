These terms govern the reservation of a numbered bottle from the NILA 150-bottle release, between you and Tirama Pte Ltd. Please read them — they cover the deposit, delivery, refunds, and the unusual realities of working with the sea.

“NILA”, “we”, “us” and “our” refer to Tirama Pte Ltd, a private company incorporated in Singapore, with its registered office at 5 Upper Aljunied Lane, #01-34, Singapore 360005. “You” refers to the person making a reservation.

By securing an allocation through nila.wine/founder-cellar you accept these terms. If you do not accept them, please do not proceed.

N° 02

The reservation

A reservation is a right to acquire a numbered bottle from the current NILA release, subject to these terms. It is not a sale of wine. We will allocate bottle numbers in the order reservations are received, with the lowest numbers going to the earliest reservations.

Each person may reserve a maximum of five (5) bottles in this release.

N° 03

Deposit and balance

The reservation deposit is SGD 198 per bottle, charged at the moment you complete checkout. The deposit is refundable in full at any time until your bottle has been retrieved from the sea and prepared for delivery (see N° 06).

The balance — representing the final bottle price minus your deposit — is settled at the moment of delivery, not before.

Payment is processed by Stripe Payments, Inc. We do not store your card details. All amounts are in Singapore Dollars (SGD) and include Singapore GST where applicable.

N° 04

Eligibility & age verification

You must be at least 18 years of age and of legal drinking age in your country of residence to reserve. By proceeding, you confirm both. We may, at any time before delivery, request reasonable proof of age. We reserve the right to refuse delivery and refund the deposit in full if proof is not provided.

N° 05

The sea aging process

Bottles are submerged at sea for approximately six months at a depth of fifteen metres in Singapore waters. The exact retrieval window is subject to weather, sea conditions, and regulatory clearance. We will communicate the expected delivery window once retrieval is complete.

In the unlikely event that all or part of the release is lost or rendered non-deliverable due to events at sea, we will offer either a full refund of your deposit or a transfer to the next release, at your election.

N° 06

Delivery

Complimentary delivery is included within Singapore. Delivery to other locations is arranged on request and quoted separately.

Delivery is hand-arranged by us or by a courier carrying a Singapore liquor licence, in compliance with the Liquor Control (Supply and Consumption) Act 2015. An adult of legal drinking age must be present to receive and sign for the delivery.

N° 07

Cancellation & refunds

You may cancel your reservation and receive a full refund of your deposit at any time before your bottle has been prepared for delivery. To cancel, write to hello@nila.wine from the email used at reservation. Refunds are issued to the original payment method within ten (10) business days.

Once a bottle has been retrieved and prepared for delivery, the reservation is no longer cancellable, but unopened bottles damaged in transit will be replaced or refunded in full at our discretion.
