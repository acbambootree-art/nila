Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 June 2026

A short, honest account of what we collect, why, and how we look after it. Compliant with Singapore’s Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA).

N° 01

Who we are

Tirama Pte Ltd — the company behind NILA — is the data controller for personal data collected via nila.wine. Our registered office is at 5 Upper Aljunied Lane, #01-34, Singapore 360005.

N° 02

What we collect

We collect only what we need, and we tell you what that is.

  • At reservation: name, email address, billing address, delivery address, phone number, and the bottle quantity reserved.
  • At payment: your payment is processed by Stripe Payments, Inc. We receive a confirmation token, the last four digits of the card, and the country of issue — never the full card number, CVC, or expiry.
  • At delivery: proof of age confirmation, and the name of the recipient who signs for the bottle.
  • From the website: standard server logs (IP address, user agent, pages viewed) for security and aggregate analytics. We do not run third-party advertising trackers.
N° 03

How we use it

  • To fulfil your reservation — allocate a bottle, deliver it, charge the balance.
  • To send service emails about your reservation (confirmation, delivery window, balance receipt).
  • To meet our legal obligations, including age verification and licensed-delivery records under Singapore’s Liquor Control (Supply and Consumption) Act 2015.
  • To prevent fraud and keep the site secure.
  • To improve the site and our service, in aggregate — never in a way that identifies you to a third party.

We do not sell your data, ever. We do not share it with advertisers.

N° 04

Who we share it with

The minimum number of carefully chosen partners required to deliver your bottle:

  • Stripe Payments, Inc. — payment processing. Stripe’s privacy policy.
  • Vercel Inc. — website hosting and server logs.
  • Licensed delivery partners — only the information needed to complete delivery (name, address, phone, recipient signature).
  • Email service provider — transactional emails about your reservation.
  • Singapore regulators — only where legally required (for example, liquor licensing records).

Each partner is bound by their own confidentiality obligations and processes data only on our instructions.

N° 05

Where we store it

Data is stored on servers operated by Stripe and Vercel, which may be located outside Singapore. Where data is transferred outside Singapore, we ensure it is protected to a standard comparable to the PDPA, in line with section 26 of the Act.

N° 06

How long we keep it

  • Reservation and delivery records: seven (7) years, in line with Singapore tax and corporate record-keeping requirements.
  • Marketing emails (if you opt in): until you unsubscribe.
  • Server logs: ninety (90) days.
N° 07

Your rights

Under the PDPA, you have the right to:

  • Ask what personal data we hold about you, and how we’ve used it.
  • Ask us to correct any information that is inaccurate.
  • Withdraw consent for any further processing — though this may mean we can’t complete your reservation.

To exercise any of these, write to hello@nila.wine. We’ll respond within thirty (30) days. There is no charge.

N° 08

Cookies

We use only the small number of first-party cookies required for the site to work and to remember your age-verification choice for the duration of your browser session. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

N° 09

Minors

NILA is offered exclusively to persons of legal drinking age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided information to us, please write to hello@nila.wine and we will remove it promptly.

N° 10

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, the updated version will be published here with a new "last updated" date. Material changes will be notified to anyone with an active reservation.
